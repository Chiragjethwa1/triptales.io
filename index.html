Glorious Goa.

Good vibes happen on the tides. A kaleidoscopic blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures, sweetened with sun, sea, sand, and seafood. Goa is known as India's pocket-sized paradise. Visit places like aga & calangute beach, panjim church and Basilica de Bom Jesus. There are activities such as Bungee jumping, scuba diving, and paragliding for adventure lovers.

Rishikesh Retreats.

One of the holiest place of India. Rishikesh is a city located in the northern Indian state of Uttarakhand. It is situated at the foothills of the Himalayas, along the banks of the holy river Ganges. Rishikesh is renowned as a spiritual and yoga center. Don't miss a chance to visit places like Ram Jhula, Triveni ghat and Neelkanth Mahadev Temple. Youth can also enjoy activities like River rafting, Sky cycling and flying fox.

Royal Rajasthan.


A land of royal heritage, diverse culture, and ancient history. Here is India's archetypal land of maharajas and medieval forts, palaces and tigers, and kaleidoscopic festivals. Rajasthan really is the jewel in India's crown. Must-visit places are Hawa Mahal, Jal Mahal, and City Palace. Activities included are Rajasthani folk dance, Desert Safari and Hot air baloon ride.

Luminous Ladakh.

Ladakh is a high-altitude desert region in the northernmost part of India. Leh is the largest city and the main tourist hub in the region. It offers a wide range of attractions, including ancient monasteries, scenic lakes, stunning landscapes like Pangon Lake, Nubra Valley and adventure activities such as Trekking, Camping and Mountain Biking.
